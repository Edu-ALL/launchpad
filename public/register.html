FAQ

  • Who is eligible for the scholarship?

    This EduALL Admission Mentoring Scholarship is for grade 11 students. Applicants must submit all required documents to be eligible.

  • How to apply?

    Make sure all documents needed are ready to attach for the scholarship that you apply for. Click on the Apply Now button on each scholarship detail on this page to submit your documents and personal data.

  • What are the requirements?

    EduALL Admission Mentoring Scholarship:
    The program is eligible for students in grade 11. As for rules, we have set requirements that include:
    - Academic transcript Grade 9 onwards
    - UN Grade 9 / IGCSE Report 
    - Letter of recommendation (minimum from one school teacher)
    - Personal Statement
    - Bank statement (in the last 3 months)
    - Activity resume (on application form)

  • What happens after the deadline?

    You will be called for an interview if you are shortlisted as a potential scholarship winner.

  • When will the scholarship be announced?

    The scholarship awardee will be announced through EduALL Launchpad Event on May 11th 2024.
    The scholarship grant letter will be sent by email.
