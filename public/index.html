Academic
Improvement

Academics focus on your performance and understanding of subjects like language, math, and science, measured through standardized tests like SAT/ACT and TOEFL/IELTS, and participation in competitions or skill-building activities such as programming. Good academic standing is crucial for university applications, with higher requirements for more prestigious institutions. Strategic approaches like selecting relevant subjects, aiming for target scores, and participating in extracurricular activities enhance academic performance and competitiveness.
